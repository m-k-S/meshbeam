BEAM SHAPER: PM780 FIBER → 15mm FLAT-TOP
==================================================

OPTICAL LAYOUT (beam propagates in +Z):
  [PM780 fiber] → [Collimator f=11mm] → [Lens 1] --100mm-- [Lens 2] → flat-top

Input fiber:     780PM, 0.12NA, 5.3um MFD
Collimator:      Off-the-shelf, f=11mm (e.g. Thorlabs F220APC-780)
                 Produces w0 ≈ 1.0mm collimated Gaussian
Wavelength:      780 nm
Refractive index:1.52 (polymerized IP-S at 780nm)

LENS 1 (Redistributor) — print on glass substrate:
  Aperture:    3.0 mm diameter
  Thickness:   0.50mm center, 0.623mm edge
  Max sag:     123.3 um
  Orientation: Flat face toward collimator (glass substrate side)
  Mount:       Aspheric face toward lens 2

LENS 2 (Collimator) — print on glass substrate:
  Aperture:    15.0 mm diameter
  Thickness:   0.50mm center, 0.977mm edge
  Max sag:     477.4 um
  Orientation: Aspheric face toward lens 1
  Mount:       Flat face (glass substrate) toward target

SPACING:
  Lens 1 to Lens 2: 100 mm (center-to-center)
  Mount in SM1 tube or 30mm cage system

PERFORMANCE:
  Collimation:    1 urad
  Uniformity:     3.9% CV (Poisson-limited)
  Flat-top from:  lens 2 exit to >200mm
  Target region:  starts 60mm from lens 2

FABRICATION NOTES:
  - Print each lens separately on a glass cover slip
  - Glass substrate = flat (plano) face of the lens
  - Use IP-S or equivalent transparent resin
  - Verify n at 780nm for your resin batch
  - Surface roughness target: <20nm RMS
  - STL files are in mm units
